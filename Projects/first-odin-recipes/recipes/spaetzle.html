Spätzle - mit Soß'

Spätzle is a really famous German food, espacilly in the southern part - mostly in swabia where it also was inveted.
It also can be eaten with Cheese and then you could say it's kinda like Mac And Cheese but It's waaay better.
The big difference between Spätzle and noodles is that its made with eggs.
Spätzle is usually just a side dish to a good roast venison etc. But if you are lazy and are looking for something delicous you may just eat it with gravy sauce. YUMMY!

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 20 mins

Servings: 2

Yield: 2 servings
